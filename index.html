The Process

From ad click to
ATS in 4 minutes.

NurseFlow.AI handles the entire acquisition loop — so your recruiters only touch qualified, high-intent candidates.

  • 01

    Targeted Facebook Ads Fire

    We deploy specialty-specific ad creatives targeting nurses in your target markets. High-converting Messenger flows capture interest instantly.

  • 02

    AI Chatbot Qualifies in Real-Time

    Every new lead enters a conversational screening flow — specialty, experience, location, and availability are confirmed within minutes.

  • 03

    Automated Follow-Up Engages

    Qualified leads receive an instant personalized SMS + email. Cold leads enter a drip sequence to re-engage over time.

  • 04

    Pipeline Lands in Your ATS

    Qualified candidates sync to Vars Health automatically — tagged, categorized, and ready for recruiter outreach. No manual data entry.
